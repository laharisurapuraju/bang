=============

   $ python app.py
   Visit: http://localhost:5000

STEP 2: Fill in the Form
   Fill regular fields:
   - Ward ID: 45
   - Rainfall: 150.5 mm
   - Temperature: 28.5°C
   - Garbage Count: 20 complaints
   - Waterlogging Count: 15 reports
   
   NEW - Optionally describe complaints:
   - Garbage: "Massive flooding and stagnant water for 5 days"
   - Waterlogging: "Persistent drainage blockage in residential area"

STEP 3: Submit & See Results
   System automatically:
   - Analyzes complaint text for severity
   - Calculates severity scores
   - Weights complaint counts based on severity
   - Uses weighted scores for predictions
   - Shows severity indicators in results

STEP 4: Interpret Results
   You'll see:
   ✓ Predicted dengue cases: 62
   ✓ Risk level: HIGH ⚠️
   ✓ Complaint severity: Garbage 10.0/10, Waterlogging 6.2/10
   ✓ Visual charts and heatmaps
