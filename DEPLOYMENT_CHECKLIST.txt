SEVERITY SCORING ALGORITHM
===========================

Step 1: Base Score (0-8)
  extreme (8.0): catastrophic, massive, severe, epidemic, outbreak, emergency
  high (6.0):    flooding, stagnant, contaminated, continuous, persistent
  medium-high:   clogged, overflowing, water, mosquito, blockage, breeding
  medium (2.0):  report, complaint, issue, problem, concern

Step 2: Multipliers
  Duration:      1.0 - 2.5x (longer = higher)
  Location:      1.0 - 1.4x (hospital=1.4x, slum=1.3x, etc)
  Health Impact: 1.0 - 1.6x (disease mentions)
  Detail Level:  1.0 - 1.3x (longer descriptions)

Step 3: Final Score
  Severity = Base × Duration × Location × Health × Detail
  (capped at 10.0)

Step 4: Weighted Score
  Weighted = Original Count × (1.0 + Severity/10.0)
