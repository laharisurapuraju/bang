CORE FUNCTIONALITY OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SEVERITY SCORING (0-10 Scale):

Step 1 - Base Score Detection (0-8 points):
  EXTREME (8.0): catastrophic, massive, severe, epidemic, outbreak
  HIGH (6.0):    flooding, stagnant, contaminated, persistent, breeding
  MED-HIGH (4.0): clogged, water, blockage, mosquito, drain
  MEDIUM (2.0):  report, complaint, issue, problem, concern

Step 2 - Multiplier Application:
  Duration:      1.0-2.5x (longer stagnation = higher risk)
  Location:      1.0-1.4x (hospital=1.4x, slum=1.3x, market=1.3x)
  Health Impact: 1.0-1.6x (disease mentions increase severity)
  Detail Level:  1.0-1.3x (longer descriptions = more serious)

Step 3 - Final Calculation:
  Severity Score = Base × Duration × Location × Health × Detail
  (Capped at 10.0)

Step 4 - Weighted Score:
  Weighted = Count × (1.0 + Severity/10.0)

EXAMPLE:
  Input: 20 complaints, "Massive flooding for 5 days in slum"
  Severity: 10.0/10
  Weighted: 20 × (1 + 10/10) = 20 × 2.0 = 40
  (vs original: 20... 2x increase in risk score!)
